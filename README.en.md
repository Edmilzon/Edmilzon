## About Me

Backend & DevOps Engineer from **Cochabamba, Bolivia**. Systems Engineering student at **Universidad Mayor de San Simón (UMSS)**.

I turn ideas into scalable solutions. My core stack: **NestJS**, **Spring Boot**, **TypeScript**, **PostgreSQL**, **Docker**, and **AWS**. I also build mobile apps with **Flutter** and frontends with **Next.js**.

Passionate about **software architecture** (hexagonal, DDD, clean architecture, SOLID). Linux expert (Arch Linux) and **DevOps** with deployments on AWS, Vercel, Railway, and Render.
